Getting Started Guide

Step 1: Create Your Account

Use the registration page to sign up with a username, email, and password.

Step 2: Log In

Enter your credentials on the login page to access your dashboard.

Step 3: Start a Session

Click “Start a New Session”, set your location, radius, cuisine, and invite friends.

Step 4: Swipe Through Options

Review each restaurant’s info and Google reviews. Like or pass to narrow down choices.

Step 5: Match and Enjoy

Once everyone likes the same spot, view the restaurant’s details and get directions.
